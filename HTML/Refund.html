4

You'll receive confirmation and the refund in your selected method.

Refund Policy

  • 100% refund if cancelled 48+ hours before departure
  • 85% refund if cancelled 24-48 hours before departure
  • 50% refund if cancelled 6-24 hours before departure
  • No refund if cancelled less than 6 hours before departure
  • Full refund for bus operator cancellations

Important Notice

Refund processing time may vary depending on your payment method. Bank transfers typically take 3-5 business days, while wallet credits are instant.
